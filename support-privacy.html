Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 2026

Data Collection

Bench Reference does not collect any personal information. We do not require account registration, login, or any personal details to use the app.

Camera & Photo Library Access

Bench Reference may request access to your device's camera and photo library. This is used solely for the My Notes feature, which allows you to attach photos to your personal notes and entries.

  • Camera and photo access is entirely optional ‚Äî the app functions fully without it
  • Photos you attach are stored locally on your device only
  • Photos are never uploaded, transmitted, or shared with us or any third party
  • You can revoke camera or photo access at any time in your device's Settings

Analytics & Tracking

The app does not use any analytics, tracking tools, or advertising frameworks. No third-party SDKs are included.

Local Storage

User preferences (such as dark mode and font size), any notes you create, and any photos you attach are stored locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to any server.

Internet Access

The app does not require an internet connection. All reference content is included within the app. The only external links are optional buy links for 3D printing resins and this support page.

Children's Privacy

The app does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. No data is collected from any user of any age.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this privacy policy, changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date.
