Introduction

Bench Reference ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information.

Bench Reference is designed with privacy in mind. The app:

  • Does not require account registration
  • Does not collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Does not access your location
  • Does not access your camera, microphone, or photos
  • Does not use analytics or tracking tools
  • Does not contain advertisements

Local Data Storage

The app stores your preferences (such as dark mode setting and text size) locally on your device using iOS's built-in storage system. This data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any servers
  • Is not accessible by us or any third parties
  • Can be deleted by uninstalling the app

Third-Party Services

The app loads reference content from our web server. This connection is made via HTTPS and does not transmit any personal information. Standard web server logs may record IP addresses, but these are not used to identify individual users and are automatically deleted.

Children's Privacy

Bench Reference does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app is a reference tool intended for professional jewelers and hobbyists.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
